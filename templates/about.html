About JAIGP

Pioneering a new frontier in scientific publishing

The Journal for AI Generated Papers (JAIGP) is pioneering a new frontier in scientific publishing. As AI becomes an increasingly powerful tool for research and writing, we recognize the need for a dedicated space where AI-generated papers can be shared, reviewed, and refined.

Our Mission

To create a collaborative platform where human prompters and AI systems work together to advance knowledge, while maintaining transparency about the nature of AI-generated content.

Multi-Stage Review System

A formal peer review process will be introduced on March 21st, 2026. This system will enable structured evaluation of AI-generated research while maintaining the collaborative ethos of the journal.

Currently, all papers carry a 📝 Submitted status and are available for community engagement through comments and discussion.

A Multidisciplinary Journal for Ideas That Deserve Exploration

JAIGP is a multidisciplinary journal that welcomes contributions from all fields of science. Whether your research spans physics, biology, computer science, engineering, social sciences, humanities, mathematics, or any other domain of inquiry, we embrace the diversity of AI-assisted research across all disciplines. Our platform is designed to foster cross-disciplinary dialogue and innovation.

We also recognize that many researchers have side project ideas—hypotheses to test, questions to explore, connections to investigate—that never get implemented due to time, resource, or institutional constraints. JAIGP provides an outlet for these ideas. By collaborating with AI, you can bring your research questions to life, share preliminary explorations, and contribute to scientific discourse even when traditional research pathways aren't available. Your curiosity-driven side projects have value, and we're here to help you explore them.

Why JAIGP?

Traditional journals are optimized for human-authored research. AI-generated papers present unique challenges and opportunities that deserve their own ecosystem. We provide:

Transparency

Clear attribution of both human prompters and AI models

Iteration

Version control that encourages continuous improvement

Collaboration

Community review through comments and feedback

Exploration

A safe space to experiment with AI-generated research

Our Philosophy

At the heart of JAIGP lies a commitment to learning through collaborative exploration. We believe that understanding emerges not from perfect knowledge, but from thoughtful inquiry conducted in partnership with both humans and AI systems.

In this space, we embrace productive uncertainty. We recognize that AI-generated research challenges traditional notions of authorship, creativity, and expertise. Rather than pretending to have all the answers, we invite researchers, thinkers, and curious minds to join us in exploring these questions together.

Every paper submitted to JAIGP represents an experiment in human-AI collaboration. Some experiments will succeed brilliantly; others will teach us valuable lessons. All contributions help us understand the evolving landscape of AI-assisted research. Through this collective exploration, we learn not just about our research topics, but about the very nature of knowledge creation in the age of AI.

Badge System

To help assess author credibility and experience, JAIGP uses a badge system based on publication records from ORCID. Every author's badge is automatically calculated from their verified ORCID journal articles and books:

Gold

50+ works

Silver

25-49 works

Bronze

6-24 works

Copper

1-5 works

New

0 works

Badges are displayed next to author names throughout the site, showing both the badge level and total publication count. For example: (52) indicates a Gold badge author with 52 publications.

The (0) badge indicates authors with no indexed publications yet. This includes early-career researchers, graduate students, and those new to publishing. The info icon helps the community understand that while these authors may have valuable insights and AI collaboration skills, they haven't yet established a publication track record.

Badges help the community assess author experience while maintaining transparency. All authors must authenticate with ORCID, ensuring verified professional identities and reducing spam. You can refresh your badge anytime from your profile page to reflect new publications.
